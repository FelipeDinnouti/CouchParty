# Wood Cutting Game Concept

A competitive reaction-based game for 2-4 players.

## Core Mechanics

- Players take turns (or play simultaneously) chopping down a tree
- Each player rapidly presses two buttons to chop
- Branches fall from above — if a branch is above your position and you chop, it falls on you and stuns you

## Controls

- **Two buttons** on the phone screen (left/right or A/B)
- Rapid alternating presses to chop faster
- Release to dodge overhead branches

## Global Screen

- Side view of a tree with players around it
- Tree depletes as players chop
- Branches visibly growing and falling
- Stun animation when a player is hit

## Scoring

- First to chop through the tree wins
- Bonus points for style (no stuns)
- Penalty time for getting hit by branches
