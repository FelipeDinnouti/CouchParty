Couch Party - A Games Platform – “Bring Your Own Phone”

Imagine this: you have friends over, you fire up your TV, and everyone jumps into a game together—no extra controllers, no app downloads, just the phones already in their pockets. That’s exactly what this project does.

How it works

· A lightweight website runs on a laptop (or any computer) in your living room.
· The TV Stick (Xiaomi, Chromecast, etc.) opens the “big screen” view so everyone can see the game.
· Every friend opens a link on their phone’s browser and it instantly becomes a custom game controller—buttons, joysticks, even motion controls.

Because everything talks over your home Wi‑Fi, the response is instant, perfect for real‑time action.

Main features

· True motion controls – tilt, swing, and point your phone like a Wii remote for bowling, sword fights, drawing, and more. The phone vibrates to give you feedback.
· Modular minigames – adding a new game is as simple as dropping a folder into the project. Games don’t interfere with each other, so you can build a library of party classics (Pong, trivia, Pictionary, air hockey…) and switch between them on the TV.
· Persistent player system – players join once, their scores carry across all minigames, creating a tournament feel for the whole evening.
· Optimised performance – gyroscope data is sent in compact binary form only when needed, keeping everything smooth even with 4–6 players flailing around.
· Zero setup for guests – just scan a QR code from the TV, tap a link, and you’re in. No accounts, no app store, no frustration.

In short, it’s a fully customisable, Wii‑Sports‑style party machine that uses only web technologies and the devices you already own. The dream: a never‑ending expandable game box for your living room.
